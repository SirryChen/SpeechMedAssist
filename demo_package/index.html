0. Preface

Text-based interaction is currently the primary communication mode for existing medical large models, allowing younger users to conveniently engage in online consultations through typing. However, in real-world scenarios such as medical consultations, voice interaction is a more direct, convenient, and elderly-friendly form of communication. Therefore, we aim to promote the application of end-to-end speech large models in medical consultation settings. We decompose the doctor’s multi-turn consultation process into the following steps: symptom analysis, proactive questioning, and providing diagnostic or treatment advice.

To evaluate the performance of existing models in medical consultation scenarios, we conducted three groups of tests:

  • Wild: We collected patient speech from real-world environments containing substantial noise to test whether the model can handle realistic conditions.
  • Single QA: We gathered a set of common questions to test whether the model possesses relevant medical knowledge through single-turn question-answering.
  • Multi Conv: We collected real patient information to drive the model to play the role of a patient and engage in multi-turn interactions with the tested model, simulating real medical consultations to evaluate its diagnostic ability.

Since our model is primarily trained on Chinese corpora, the following results are mainly presented in Chinese.

0. 前置说明

文本交互是现有医疗大模型的主要交互方式，便于年轻群体直接在网络中通过打字的方式进行交流。 但在实际的场景中，例如医疗咨询，语音交互是更为直接、便捷、老年人友好的交互方式。 因此，我们期望推动端到端的语音大模型在医疗咨询场景下的应用。 我们将医生在多轮咨询过程中的步骤拆解为：分析病症、主动追问、给出诊断或治疗意见。

为验证现有的模型在医疗咨询场景下的性能，我们进行了三组测试：

  • Wild：收集真实场景下的患者语音，包含大量噪声，测试模型能否应对真实环境；
  • Single QA：我们收集了一些常见的问题，通过单轮问答的方式测试模型是否具备相关的医疗知识；
  • Multi Conv：我们收集真实患者的信息，用于驱动大模型扮演患者的角色，与待测试的模型进行多轮的交互，模拟真实环境下的医疗咨询过程，用于测试模型的诊断能力。

- 产后子宫收缩乏力，无法有效止血，可能与激素水平波动、贫血或感染有关。

3. **感染风险**
- 长期出血易增加子宫内膜炎等感染风险，需警惕发热、腹痛、分泌物异味等症状。

4. **其他原因**
- 凝血功能障碍、宫颈损伤或剖宫产伤口愈合不良等，但概率较低。

1. **尽快复查彩超**
